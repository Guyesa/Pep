Become our partner

Partnerships are the heart-line of Pastoralist Empowerment Pillars (PEP). PEP refers as PARTNERS to all those individuals, communities and organizations which join hands for extending program interventions not only through financial support but also through capacity building from different perspectives.

Why partner with PEP?

PEP has better understanding of local community as it enhance the breadth and depth of participation by community residents in local decision making.
Benefit of partnering with PEP
• Balance the most involved advocates with perspectives representing more of the community
• Identify broader community-based resources and recommendations
• Develop communication channels for keeping people informed over time
• Enhance the cultural competency of engagement plans and increase the ability to translate issues into relevant questions/framing and accessible language
• Reach people emotionally as well as physically
Inform consult involve collaborate empower
Common examples of PEP partner tasks include:
• Expand awareness of upcoming public engagement processes
• Co-host public input sessions in locations more familiar to community members
• Help understand communities current level of understanding about an issue so materials can provide helpful background context
• Help adopt information about the issue into language and format that make sense to nontechnical experts and people with varying levels of education
• Translate information and provide bilingual facilitation if appropriate
• Help with reporting back to the community about how their input was used in the final decision and ways that they can stay involved and informed
